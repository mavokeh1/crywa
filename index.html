The Passionate Distillers

Crywan Distillers Ltd is a producer of quality and portable spirits. Established and registered on 2007 under the Companies Act, the company has heavily invested in innovating various brands to meet different consumer needs.

Crywan Enterprises Ltd has been in business since 2007 and hasgrown to have a reach of 40 out of 47 counties in Kenya whereour products are on sale.

This has been made possible from our first flagship product - Kingston master Vodka, and moving on to build a portfolio of more than 5 quality and portable spirits i.e. Vennice vodka, Visa vodka, Mara ice and Grand label Whisky
